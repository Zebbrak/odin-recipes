Pasta Bolognese

Description

This is a lovely take on a simple dish, pasta bolognese.

This recipe only needs a few ingredients and only takes about 45 min to finish

Ingredients

  • Pasta Fettucini
  • Ground Beef
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Basil
  • Onion
  • Garlic

Steps

  1. Bring water to a boil in a big pot and add the pasta
  2. Dice onion and garlic and brown on the stove
  3. Add ground beef and cook together with onion and garlic, add salt & pepper as you please
  4. Once the ground beef is browned, add tomato sauce and let it simmer for 10-15 min
  5. Add basil
  6. Once the pasta is al-dente drain from the pot of water
  7. Serve the pasta, add the bolognese ontop
  8. Sprinkle as much or as little parmesan cheese as you please ontop
  9. Add some more basil for flavour if you dare
  10. Enjoy your meal!
