Meatloaf and Mash Potatoes with Brocoli and Gravy

Delicious and easy to make meatloaf with mashed potatoes

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Ground Beef
  • Brocoli
  • Gravy
  • Butter
  • Milk
  • Onion
  • Breadcrumbs

Steps

  1. Dice onions and mix together with ground beef, breadcrumbs, milk and seasoning
  2. Peel and boil the potatoes until soft
  3. Form the ground beef into a meatloaf and preheat the oven to 350 degrees
  4. Bake the meatloaf for 30-45 minutes
  5. Drain the potatoes and mash them in a bowl, add butter and milk and salt to taste
  6. Cook the brocoli, e.g steam, boil, bake of fry
  7. In a seperate pot cook the gravy, add some sauce from the meatloaf pan
  8. Slice the meatloaf and serve with mashed potatoes, brocoli and gravy
