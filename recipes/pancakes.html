Pancakes

This is Swedish style pancakes filled with sugar and jam ontop

These pancakes are easy to make and very thin but yet filling!

Ingredients

  • Flower
  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Add flower, milk and egg in a bowl and whisk it together to a fine smooth consistency
  2. Add some salt and melted butter into the bowl
  3. Heat a frying pan on low heat and add some butter
  4. Add roughly 1/2 cup of the bowl into the pan
  5. After approximately 2 min flip the pancake and finish it off for another minute
  6. Fill the pancake with sugar, ice cream, jam, nutella or whatever else you please and roll the pancake up
  7. Enjoy!
